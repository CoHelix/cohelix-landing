About the Pilot

We want to make Cohelix as useful as possible to all hospitality and retail businesses. But we can only do this through your feedback.

Cohelix is currently in development. However, when it is ready we’d love for you to try Cohelix and hear what you think.

We’ll let you use Cohelix free of charge for a month. In return we’ll spend 30 mins with you each week to get your feedback on how Cohelix works for you. Your input will enable us to optimise the product to better meet your needs.

And to say thank you for your help, you'll receive the first three months of Cohelix for free.

There is no commitment. You can withdraw your interest in the pilot programme at any time.

If you have any questions please do not hesitate to contact us.
